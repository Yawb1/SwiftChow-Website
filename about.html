About SWIFT CHOW

Ghana's Favorite Fast Food Since 2020

Our Story

SWIFT CHOW was born in 2020 in the heart of Accra, Ghana, from a simple dream: to bring world-class fast food to Ghanaians at affordable prices, without compromising on quality or taste.

What started as a small kitchen in Osu has grown into a beloved brand serving thousands of customers across Ghana. Our founder, inspired by both international flavors and local Ghanaian tastes, created a menu that speaks to everyone.

Today, SWIFT CHOW continues to grow, but our commitment remains the same: fresh ingredients, fast service, and food that brings people together.

Our Mission

To provide delicious, affordable, and hygienic fast food to every Ghanaian, delivered with speed and a smile. We believe everyone deserves to enjoy quality food without breaking the bank.

Our Vision

To become West Africa's most loved fast food brand, known for quality, innovation, and exceptional customer experience. We aim to have a SWIFT CHOW outlet in every major city by 2030.

Our Values

What drives us every day

Quality First

We never compromise on the quality of our ingredients. From fresh vegetables to premium meats, only the best makes it to your plate.

Hygiene & Safety

Our kitchens follow strict international hygiene standards. Your health and safety are our top priorities.

Speed & Efficiency

We value your time. That's why we've perfected our processes to deliver hot, fresh food in record time.

Customer Love

Our customers are family. We listen, we adapt, and we always strive to exceed your expectations.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our story

2020

The Beginning

SWIFT CHOW opens its first kitchen in Osu, Accra, serving just 5 menu items with a dream to feed Ghana.

2022

10,000 Orders

We hit our first major milestone — 10,000 orders delivered. Our menu expanded to 20+ items.

2024

Going Digital

Launch of online ordering, mobile app, and delivery tracking. Expanded to 5 cities across Ghana.

2026

50,000+ Customers

Now serving 10 cities, 44+ menu items, and loved by over 50,000 happy customers across Ghana.

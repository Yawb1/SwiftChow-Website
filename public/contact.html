Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Sunday. Orders placed before 9:30 PM will be delivered the same day.

Which cities do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to Accra only. Delivery to Tema, Kumasi, Takoradi, Cape Coast, Tamale, and other major cities is coming soon! Stay tuned for updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery, Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), and Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard).

How long does delivery take?

Typical delivery time is 30-45 minutes within city centers. During peak hours or for locations further out, it may take up to 60 minutes.

Do you cater for events?

Yes! We offer catering services for parties, corporate events, and special occasions. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance for large orders.
